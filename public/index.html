The Western Ghat Farmer Producer Company

We are a farmers collective owned and managed by farmers. We promote nature friendly cultivation in the eco sensitive Western Ghat region. Our goal is to empower farmers to use sustainable farming techniques while ensuring fair value for their produce.

What We Do

The Western Ghat region is one of the bio diverse parts of the world with a variety of unique plants that are native to this region. Quite a number of these plants and their products are high in nutritional and medicinal properties. At WEFPCO, we hope to promote the organic and natural cultivation of these plants in a sustainable manner without the use of harmful chemicals and pesticides. Our primary focus is Moringa (Moringa Olifiera) cultivation and the production of products derived from this super food.

Our activities and services

Empower -> Sustain -> Grow Together

  • Training in Eco-friendly nature farming methods
  • Provide Technical Know-how for farmers
  • Provide seeds and Eco-friendly fertilizers
  • Arrange purchases of Farming equipments
  • Marketing support for farmers
  • Classes on why protecting the environment benefits the farmer
  • Arrange field trips to various farms in the region
  • Guidance and support for farmers in the production of value added items
  • Soil examination support and recommendations to improve soil quality
  • Regular follow ups with partnered farmers and information sharinh
